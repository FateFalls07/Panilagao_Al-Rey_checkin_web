Baggage Tracking and Assistance

You may approach the Baggage Assistance Counter located at the Arrival Area for any inquiries regarding your baggage

If no claim for NR tagged on-hand baggage is made within five (5) days after the baggage was found, NAL will turnover unclaimed baggage to Customs for disposal.

If the baggage is not found by the seventh day after the baggage was reported missing then the baggage shall be considered lost and necessary claim shall be filed.

Report immediately any baggage incident that you may encounter during your arrival.
